# Testing Strategy

These questions are designed to test whether the structure and design of the site make its purpose and main artifact clear to users.

1. After viewing the homepage for about 15 seconds, can you identify the main research question of the site?

2. Can you tell what the project is about from the homepage alone, without opening the other pages?

3. Can you find the network visualization easily without being told where it is?

4. Is it clear what the network visualization represents after reading the findings page?

5. Can you move between the pages easily using the navigation menu?

6. I was unsure whether placing the network visualization at the center of the findings page would make it the clear focal point of the site. Did that design choice work for you?
